What exactly is search engine optimization ?

SEO is the process of upgrading the contents of a website so as to increase its visibility during an organic search on search engines as well as the volume and quality of organic (unpaid) traffic to the website. Search engines have different algorithms that help to determine if a website is worthy of high ranking on the Search Engine Result Pages (SERP), search engines (Google, Bing, Baidu, Yandex, etc) are able to crawl into website pages so as to determine whether or not the contents of such sites are important to the query made by search engine users per time.

There are certain ways through which optimization is carried out by digital marketing experts, this are done with search engine algorithm in mind so as to ensure a favorable position on the SERP and improve the user experience of site visitors. They include the following

Pages Keywords

Keywords are phrases or words used in web content that help the website rank higher via search engines. Carefully choosing keywords with high search volume and low competition, and effectively using them in content is a good SEO practice that helps a site rank high.

Pages content

A website should have relevant and educative contents; contents that answer the questions asked by a user/visitor, also contents should be both attractive and shareable, contents should come in different forms that can appeal to a wide range of audience. These forms include

On-page SEO

This is the practice of optimizing a site using location based keywords. This form of optimization can be done by creating location specific pages on your site, creating business pages on Google Maps, claiming directory listings, etc. this form of optimization helps you rank within the location of your business.

Off-page SEO

This is a form of optimization that happens outside of the website; these are activities that happen away from your web page but have a link to your site. These activities include building of backlinks, commenting, sharing on other platforms, and star rating /liking. These activities play a crucial role in increasing the rating of a site.
